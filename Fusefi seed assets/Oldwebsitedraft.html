FAQ

Common questions

Have a special request? Ask — we can tailor kits for your venue and crowd size.

How fast is the setup?

Most customers are online in under 2 minutes: power on the kit, wait for connection, and share the Wi-Fi details on the included card.

Can I add more data during the event?

Yes. If you hit your plan limit, you can purchase add-on data packs from your dashboard (or enable auto top-ups).

What carriers do you use?

We choose the best available carrier coverage for your event location, and premium kits support multi-carrier failover.

What if I don't have a great cellular signal?

Tell us the venue address. We'll recommend the right kit (including external antennas or bonded options) and help you de-risk connectivity.
